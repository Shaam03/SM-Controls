About Us

S M Controls established in the year 2019, The panel supplier company is located in Bengaluru, Karnataka and has transformed into a dynamic organization in the field of integration, courtesy of unwavering commitment to the customer's needs.

Mission:

To rightly identify the precise business needs of our clients and fulfill their needs by providing the highest quality products and services in Power, Control & Automation solutions. Our objective is to become a trusted partner to our clients by providing impeccable service with a commitment to deliver absolute satisfaction to our customers.

Vision:

To be an admired market leader in Power, Control & Automation solutions and to create a nice for ourselves in the market as a reliable and a trusted value adding solution provider.
